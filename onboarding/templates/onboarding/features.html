Precision Modeling Powered by a Universe of Data and Advanced Analytics. Each feature is designed to work in concert. Together, they provide a complete picture of your solar potential through irradiance analysis, system modeling, and predictive analytics.

Astronomical Data

Our astronomical data Features provide precise calculations of sun position, sunrise/sunset times, solar noon, and daylight duration to optimize solar energy production.

  • Sun path tracking
  • Seasonal variations
  • Azimuth & elevation angles
  • Sunrise/sunset times
  • Solar noon calculations
  • Daylight duration analysis

Weather Data Integration

Real-time weather conditions, cloud cover analysis, temperature, and atmospheric pressure integration for accurate solar forecasting.

  • Cloud cover forecasting
  • Temperature adjustments
  • Precipitation impact analysis
  • Atmospheric pressure monitoring
  • Humidity factor calculations
  • Wind speed & direction analysis

Irradiance Analysis

Comprehensive measurements of Global Horizontal Irradiance (GHI), Direct Normal Irradiance (DNI), and Diffuse Horizontal Irradiance (DHI) for precise solar energy assessment.

  • Real-time irradiance data
  • Historical patterns
  • Spectral analysis
  • GHI, DNI & DHI measurements
  • Irradiance forecasting
  • Site-specific analysis

PVWatts Modelling

Integration with NREL's PVWatts API for accurate energy production forecasts based on system parameters and environmental conditions.

  • System capacity modeling
  • Tilt & azimuth optimization
  • Loss factor calculations
  • Array type configuration
  • Shading analysis
  • Performance ratio estimation

Detailed Analytics

Comprehensive reports with financial analysis, environmental impact assessment, and performance metrics to maximize your solar investment.

  • Financial ROI calculations
  • Carbon offset tracking
  • Performance scoring
  • Energy production reports
  • System health monitoring
  • Maintenance scheduling

Machine Learning Algorithms

Advanced algorithms including SARIMA, XGBoost, and neural networks achieving over 90% forecast accuracy for solar energy production.

  • Feature engineering

Solar energy modelling solutions for every scale and application

We deliver precise solutions designed to meet specific needs across residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Our technology helps optimize energy usage, improve system performance, and maximize returns on solar investments.

Residential Users

Our residential modeling solution provides homeowners with accurate energy production estimates for their solar systems. By understanding exactly how much energy your panels will produce under different conditions, you can make informed decisions about system sizing and energy usage patterns.

For homes considering solar investments, our modeling tools help evaluate different system configurations, panel types, and installation angles to maximize energy production and financial returns. We provide clear, understandable reports that show expected energy output and cost savings.

The result is confident investment decisions, optimized system design, and realistic expectations about solar energy production for your specific location and home characteristics.

  • Accurate energy production estimates
  • System sizing guidance
  • Financial return calculations
  • Location-specific analysis
  • Simple, user-friendly interface

Commercial Buildings

For commercial facilities, our modeling solutions provide detailed energy production analysis to support investment decisions and system design. We model performance under various conditions to give businesses confidence in their solar projects.

Our system analyzes multiple factors including roof orientation, shading, local climate patterns, and energy consumption profiles to deliver precise production estimates. This helps businesses evaluate the financial viability and operational impact of solar installations.

For existing solar systems, our tools provide performance benchmarking and help identify optimization opportunities through detailed analysis of actual vs. expected production.

  • Detailed production analysis
  • Financial modeling
  • ROI calculations
  • System optimization
  • Performance reporting

Solar Installation Companies

Our specialized modeling tools for solar installers provide accurate system performance estimates using industry-standard PVWatts and advanced PVlib modeling. We help design optimal solar solutions for each unique location and customer need.

During the design phase, our software enables rapid comparison of different system configurations, panel orientations, and component selections. This helps installers create proposals that maximize energy production and financial returns for their clients.

We incorporate multiple data sources including NASA climate data, local weather patterns, and astronomical calculations to deliver comprehensive system modeling that accounts for real-world conditions.

  • PVWatts and PVlib modeling
  • System configuration comparison
  • Production estimates
  • Technical proposal support
  • Customer education tools

Researchers & Analysts

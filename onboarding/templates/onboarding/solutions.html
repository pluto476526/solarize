Our Solutions

Solar energy modelling solutions for every scale and application

We deliver precise solutions designed to meet specific needs across residential, commercial, and industrial applications. Our technology helps optimize energy usage, improve system performance, and maximize returns on solar investments.

Residential Users

Our residential forecasting solution helps homeowners optimize their energy consumption patterns based on predicted solar generation. By understanding exactly when your solar panels will produce the most energy, you can schedule high-energy activities like running appliances, charging electric vehicles, or heating water during peak production hours.

For homes with battery storage systems, our forecasts enable intelligent charging and discharging decisions. The system automatically stores excess solar energy when generation exceeds consumption and uses stored energy when solar production is low, reducing reliance on the grid and maximizing self-consumption of your solar energy.

The result is lower electricity bills, extended battery lifespan through optimized cycling, and reduced carbon footprint by minimizing grid electricity consumption.

  • Personalized energy usage recommendations
  • Battery storage optimization
  • Maximized solar self-consumption
  • Reduced electricity costs
  • Simple, user-friendly interface

Commercial Buildings

For commercial facilities, our forecasting solutions provide the intelligence needed to align energy consumption with solar generation patterns. By predicting solar output with high accuracy, businesses can schedule energy-intensive operations during peak production periods and reduce consumption when solar generation is low.

Our system integrates with building management systems to automatically adjust HVAC settings, lighting levels, and other controllable loads based on forecasted solar availability. This synchronization between energy production and consumption significantly reduces demand charges and overall electricity costs.

For facilities considering solar investments, our forecasting tools provide accurate projections of system performance and financial returns under various scenarios, helping decision-makers evaluate the business case with confidence.

  • Demand charge reduction
  • Automated load shifting
  • ROI optimization for solar investments
  • Integration with building management systems
  • Comprehensive performance reporting

Solar Installation Companies

Our specialized tools for solar installers provide accurate system performance estimates that help design optimal solar solutions for each unique location and customer need. By incorporating hyperlocal weather data, historical patterns, and site-specific factors, we deliver precise energy production forecasts that account for seasonal variations and microclimate effects.

During the design phase, our software enables rapid comparison of different system configurations, panel orientations, and component selections. This helps installers create proposals that maximize energy production and financial returns for their clients while ensuring system reliability and longevity.

Post-installation, our monitoring and verification tools compare actual performance against forecasts, helping identify underperforming systems and providing data-driven insights for maintenance and optimization recommendations.

  • Accurate system performance estimates
  • Optimal system design guidance
  • Proposal generation support
  • Performance verification tools
  • Customer satisfaction through accurate expectations

Researchers & Analysts

For academic institutions, research organizations, and energy analysts, we provide access to comprehensive solar data and advanced analytical tools. Our platform offers historical solar radiation data, real-time measurements, and forecast models that support a wide range of research applications from climate studies to renewable energy integration analysis.

Researchers can access granular data at various temporal and spatial resolutions, enabling detailed analysis of solar resource variability, cloud impact studies, and long-term trend analysis. Our API allows seamless integration with existing research workflows and computational models.

Energy analysts and policy researchers benefit from our regional and national-scale forecasting capabilities, which support grid integration studies, renewable energy policy development, and market analysis. The data helps quantify the value of solar forecasting in different applications and geographic contexts.

  • Comprehensive historical solar data
  • High-resolution forecasting models
  • API access for integration
  • Custom data extraction tools
  • Technical support for research projects

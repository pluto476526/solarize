Our Technology

Solarize is powered by advanced algorithms, statistical models, machine learning, and real-time weather data to forecast solar energy production.

Statistical Forecasting

We use open source SARIMA, ARIMA and XGBoost models to analyze historical patterns and time series data, delivering forecasts with 95% accuracy.

Weather Data Integration

We integrate with NASA irradiance data and WeatherAPI to adjust forecasts based on changing conditions, cloud cover, and atmospheric factors.

Dashboard Analytics

Use interactive time series charts and analytics dashboard to monitor multiple locations and optimize energy production across your portfolio.
